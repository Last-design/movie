off campus

Off Campus is a romantic drama series based on the bestselling book series by Elle Kennedy. Set at Briar University, the story follows a group of college students as they navigate love, friendship, academic pressure, and the challenges of growing into adulthood.

Ready or Not

Following the shocking events of the first film, Ready or Not 2: Here I Come brings back the intense blend of horror, suspense, and dark comedy that made the original a fan favorite. The story continues after Grace survived a deadly game of hide-and-seek that turned into a fight for survival against a wealthy and mysterious family bound by a terrifying tradition.

Avatar the way of water

Avatar: The Way of Water returns audiences to the breathtaking world of Pandora, continuing the story of Jake Sully and Neytiri more than a decade after the events of the first film. Now parents, they have built a family and are determined to protect their children from new dangers that threaten their peaceful life.

the bluff

The Bluff is an action-adventure film set in the 19th century that follows a determined woman fighting to protect her family and secure her future in a dangerous and unforgiving world. Living on a remote island, she is forced to confront threats from her past when a group of ruthless enemies arrives, putting everything she loves at risk.
